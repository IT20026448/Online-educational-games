About Online Educational Games


This project was designed by us to make learning for younger children fun and effective. Research has shown us that younger children who use educational video games perform better in certain academic areas than children that only use traditional methods of learning. Our website includes access to math games, science games, english games and many more for several age groups. We hope that teachers will be able to use our website to bring educational games to their classrooms, to this end we have included several tools for teachers, such as creating classrooms and assigning a specific game to a specific student, to help teachers better manage their students.
